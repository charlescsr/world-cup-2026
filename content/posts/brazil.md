# My ones to watch

## Endrick

Someone who really found it hard to fit in at his dream club in Real Madrid and he only got a good chunk of sub appearances and could not nail a starting spot and so because of that he was sent on loan to Lyon and he was really a fairly solid player for them. # The lineup

Seems like Don Carlo prefers a 4-3-3 with this Brazil team for the most part and he can always switch to a classic 4-4-2 or a 4-2-3-1 but I think a 4-3-3 should work for this team really given the quality of wingers these guys have got. 

I think the easy choices here look to be the keeper and the CBs. Alisson is better than Ederson honestly but he is very injury prone and missed a huge chunk of Liverpool's horror title defense this season due to injury. So Ederson would get the nod. In the CBs I think you cannot look past Champions League winner and current season Champions League finalist Marquinhos and newly crowned Premier League winner and also Champions League finalist Gabriel. You get a good level of footedness with this setup and as for the RB you already know Wesley should get the pick over another CB in probably Ibanez who has been picked to play RB for Brazil from what I see but my pick should be Wesley as the young up and comer. As for the left I am most definitely NOT picking Alex Sandro as you know so the only younger option seems to be a 32 year old Douglas Santos who has had a fairly good season for Zenit over in Russia. So that is how the defense looks like.

Coming down to midfield I think Casemiro and Bruno G are most definitely lock ins. The former having a storming season with 9 goals and 2 assists from midfield as he saw out his contract at Man United and the latter having a very solid season despite his club Newcastle United performing horribly as they did not qualify for Europe but his numbers were decent really. 9 goals and 7 assists from there is a good season and he may have suitors in the summer given Newcastle are not in Europe anymore. As for the 3rd midfield option it would appear to be Danilo from Botafogo who has had a very solid couple seasons for them after his move to Nottingham Forest did not work out well and he seems to be a good scorer with 10 goals and 3 assists in the current year so I feel like he would be a good pick there.

The frontline seems to pick itself really. Vini Jr has had a fairly decent return despite his club Real Madrid ending up trophyless this season with 23 goals and 11 assists and Don Carlo loves him from his time at Real Madrid anyway. I just hope he can seriously bring this level of form to the national team because it seems to somehow disappear when he dons the Brazil shirt. The right side will be taken by Raphinha who has put up numbers for Barcelona once again with 21 goals and 7 assists as they won the title this season. As for the striker I think you cannot look past Igor Thiago. The 24 year old has had an amazing resurrection season this year with 25 goals and 1 assist after his first season for Brentford was curtailed with an ACL injury and he absolutely deserves the call up. I know it isn't exactly fear inducing given he plays for Brentford but they were very very close to qualifying for Europe too given they lost so many star talent in the summer of this season.
